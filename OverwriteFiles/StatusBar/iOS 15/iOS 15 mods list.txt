Folders names with descriptions for each mod.

- *StatBar Battery Icon 15: Hides the battery icon.*

- *StatBar Battery-Time 15: Hides the battery icon, and time.*

- *StatBar Carrier Name-Bar 15: Hides the carrier name/bars.*

- *StatBar Carrier Name-Bars-Time 15: Hides the carrier name/bars. and time.*

- *StatBar Carrier Name-Bars-Time-Battery 15: Hides the carrier name/bars, time, and the battery icon.*

- *StatBar Time 15: Hides the time in the status bar.*

- *StatBar Hide all 15: Hides the entire status bar.*
